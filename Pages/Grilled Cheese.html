A grilled cheese recipe by Alex Nguyen!!!

Description

This guide will showcase the step in making a grilled cheese sandwich with minimal ingredients and time.

Ingredients list

  1. Whole wheat or white bread.(depending on preference)
  2. 2 slices of cheese per portion
  3. Spatula
  4. Pan with stove top

Steps on making a grilled cheese sandwich

  1. Preheat the pan to medium temperature.
  2. Peel back 2 pieces of cheese
  3. Put slices of cheese onto one piece of bread
  4. Stack the other piece of bread on top of the original piece of bread and cheese
  5. Put sandwich onto grill until golden brown
  6. Flip sandwich and press until golden brown
  7. Serve and enjoy
