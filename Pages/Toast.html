How to make Toast by Alex Nguyen

Ingredients

  1. Toast
  2. Butter
  3. Maple Syrup
  4. Pan

How to make toast

  1. Spread butter on both sides of the piece of toast
  2. Put toast on preheated oven and wait till golden brown on the underside
  3. Flip the piece of toast when golden brown
  4. Once complete, top off with maple syrup and butter for added flavor
  5. Enjoy your toast!
